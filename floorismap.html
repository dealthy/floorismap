The Floor is a Map!
Explore the rivers, ridges, and towns of central Pennsylvania along the Susquehanna corridor. The specific corridor illustrated here is the same extent on the 2nd floor of the Walker Building. The tiles are an abstract of the real world features portrayed on this map. The Susquehanna river corridor, in this case, focuses on the southern central Pennsylvania featuring portion of Juniata and Susquehanna River.
